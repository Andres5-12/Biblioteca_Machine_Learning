What is CRISP-ML?

CRISP-ML is a lifecycle methodology for machine learning projects. It is designed to help teams move from a business problem to a deployable data product in a structured way.

Core Phases Implemented

  • Business Understanding: Define the project objectives, business questions, and success criteria for public library analytics.
  • Data Understanding: Explore the available open datasets, validate data quality, and understand the information contained in each source.
  • Data Engineering: Ingest, clean, normalize, integrate, and enrich data to create a reliable dataset for future analytics and modeling tasks.

How the methodology applies here

For this project, the first three CRISP-ML phases are implemented in Flask as separate documentation pages. The dataset comes from Colombian open library networks, and the current work focuses on preparing the data for later modeling.

  • We capture the business motivation behind the library analysis.
  • We describe the available data sources and the most important attributes.
  • We explain the data engineering pipeline that transforms these raw sources into a unified dataset.

Next Phase

The next CRISP-ML steps beyond this application are modeling, evaluation, deployment, and monitoring. These phases will be easier once the dataset is standardized and the business goals are clearly defined.
