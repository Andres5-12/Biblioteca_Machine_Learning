Project Context

This machine learning project is focused on public library networks in Colombia. It is intended to support strategic decisions by public library leaders and cultural policy teams.

Business Objectives

  • Identify opportunities to improve library services through better understanding of user behavior.
  • Support planning for budget allocation, program design, and infrastructure improvements.
  • Provide actionable insights for library networks in departments such as Bolívar, Cundinamarca, and Casanare.

Key Questions

  • Which regions and municipalities require more digital access or technology support in libraries?
  • How can library networks optimize cultural programming based on actual community needs?
  • What are the most relevant performance indicators for library operations and user engagement?

Success Criteria

  • Clear definition of the target business goals and data requirements.
  • Documented data sources and their relevance to the library analytics use case.
  • Creation of a clean and integrated dataset that is ready for future modeling.
