Quality and Structure

The available data includes library names, locations, contact information, service types, and some operational indicators such as internet availability and collection volume.

  • Column names are normalized to a common snake_case format.
  • Regional labels often include accents and inconsistent capitalization, requiring standardization.
  • Numeric values may be stored as text and require coercion to proper numeric types.
